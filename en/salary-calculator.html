❓ FAQ

How to calculate net from gross salary? ▾
Multiply gross by 0.77 (employee, ~23% contributions). Example: 2000€ gross × 0.77 = 1540€ net.
What are payroll contributions? ▾
Social contributions include health insurance, pension, unemployment insurance. They represent 22-27% of gross salary depending on contract type.
How to convert annual to monthly salary? ▾
Divide annual gross by 12. Example: 30,000€/year ÷ 12 = 2,500€/month gross.
