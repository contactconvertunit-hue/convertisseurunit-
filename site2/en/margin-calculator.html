❓ FAQ

What is the margin rate? ▾
Margin rate = ((Selling price - Purchase price) / Purchase price) × 100. It measures profit relative to cost.
What is the markup rate? ▾
Markup rate = ((Selling price - Purchase price) / Selling price) × 100. It measures profit relative to selling price.
What is a good margin rate? ▾
It depends on the sector. Retail: 30-50%. Services: 50-80%. Food: 60-70%. Online sales: 20-40%.
