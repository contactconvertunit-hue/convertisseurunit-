❓ FAQ

What is VAT? ▾
VAT (Value Added Tax) is a consumption tax applied to goods and services. In most European countries, the standard rate is 20%.
How to calculate VAT? ▾
To get the price incl. VAT: multiply the ex-VAT price by (1 + VAT rate). Example: 100€ × 1.20 = 120€ inc-VAT.
How to remove VAT from a price? ▾
Divide the inc-VAT price by (1 + VAT rate). Example: 120€ ÷ 1.20 = 100€ ex-VAT.
